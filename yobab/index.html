About Municipality of Agoo

Agoo is one of the oldest towns in La Union, steeped in history and tradition. Known for its artistic heritage, religious significance, and beautiful coastal landscapes, Agoo attracts visitors seeking authentic Filipino culture and natural beauty. The town is famous for the spectacular Basilica Minore of Our Lady of Charity and its vibrant community celebrations.

Why Visit Agoo?

Rich Religious Heritage

Home to the spectacular Basilica Minore, one of the largest churches in Northern Luzon, showcasing stunning architecture and spiritual significance.

Beautiful Beaches

Enjoy pristine coastal views, relaxing beaches, and stunning sunsets perfect for families and nature enthusiasts.

Vibrant Culture

Experience authentic Ilocano traditions, festivals, and celebrations that reflect the rich cultural heritage of the town.
