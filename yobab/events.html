Festivals & Events

Experience the vibrant culture of Municipality of Agoo, La Union

Annual Celebrations

Dinengdeng Festival

The Dinengdeng Festival celebrates the town’s Ilocano culture and cuisine. Street dancing, cultural performances, and food exhibits highlight the famous Ilocano dish "dinengdeng".

Celebrated: Month of May

Location: Agoo Town Proper, La Union

Agoo Town Fiesta (Feast of Our Lady of Charity)

This religious celebration honors Our Lady of Charity, the patron saint of Agoo. It includes processions, masses, cultural events, and community gatherings.

Celebrated: May 4–5

Location: Basilica Minore of Our Lady of Charity, Agoo, La Union
